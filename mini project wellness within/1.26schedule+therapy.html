HERE IS A DAILY SCHEDULE TO MAKE YOUR HEALTH BETTER

for working day

  • 7:00 AM - Wake Up:
    1. Practice deep breathing or mindfulness for a few minutes.
    2. Express gratitude: Write down three things you're thankful for.
  • 7:30 AM - Breakfast:
    1. Eat a balanced breakfast to nourish your body.
  • 8:30 AM - Morning Routine:
    1. Shower and get dressed.
    2. Set realistic goals for the day.
  • 12:00 PM - Lunch:
    1. Have a healthy and mindful meal.
  • 1:00 PM - Nature Break:
    1. Spend time outdoors, whether it's a walk in the park or simply sitting in the sun.
  • 2:00 PM - Relaxation/Meditation:
    1. Practice relaxation techniques or guided meditation
  • 6:00 PM - Light Exercise:
    1. Engage in gentle exercise, like yoga or a short workout.
  • 7:00 PM - Dinner:
    1. Enjoy a nutritious dinner.
  • 8:00 PM - Self-Care Time:
    1. Take a warm bath, read a book, or engage in another soothing activity.
  • 9:30 PM - Wind Down:
    1. Create a calming bedtime routine: dim lights, limit screen time.
    2. Reflect on positive aspects of the day.
  • 10:00 PM - Sleep:
    1. Aim for 7-8 hours of restful sleep.
